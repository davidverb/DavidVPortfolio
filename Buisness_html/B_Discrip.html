Welcome to the Happy Hound Activity Center Home Page!

We are decicated to offering a great time for your canine companion when you can't be there, all while tailoring to their needs.

Complete Cainine Care

Our services include:

  • Day care - We provide day care services in which your pup can interact with other dogs, all while exercising. Activities include walks with our staff, playing fetch, and swimming activities *Requires Owner's Approval*. For your dogs safety, we only put dogs of similiar size catagories together.

  • Boarding - If you need to go out of town, we offer boarding services. We will accomodate your pup so they can enjoy their visit. We can also provide "pup-dates" of your dog for ease of mind.

  • Veterinarian Service - We have a veterinarian on-site 24-hours a day. We also offer standard appointments so you can have a happy, healthy dog.

  • Adoption Services - If you are interested in adopting a canine in need of a loving home, please come by and check our adoption center. If you cannot stop by, then please consider signing-up to our newsletter. Along with weekly updates to our activity calendar, it also has an adoption section thats showcases pictures of our current pups in need of a loving family.
